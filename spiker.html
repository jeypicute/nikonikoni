SPIKER

1. Approach and Timing
The spiker's approach should be explosive, allowing for maximum vertical jump.

Perfect timing with the setter is critical to connect well with the ball.

2. Jumping Ability
A strong vertical leap gives a spiker the height needed to hit the ball at a downward angle, making it harder to defend.

Spikers work on both jump power and height to gain an advantage over blockers.

3. Arm Swing Technique
A quick, powerful arm swing is essential for hitting the ball with speed.

Proper arm mechanics involve leading with the elbow and finishing with a snapping motion of the wrist.
