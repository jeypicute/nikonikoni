LIBERO

1. Serve Receive (Passing)

Objective Ensure accurate, controlled passes to the setter after receiving a serve.

Key Technique Maintain a low, balanced stance, use forearm passing, and position your platform correctly to direct the ball accurately.

Drill Serve-receive drills where the libero receives different types of serves (float, topspin, etc.).

2. Defense (Digging)

Objective Defend against spikes by getting under the ball and keeping it in play.

3. Quick Reflexes and Agility

Objective Move quickly to cover the court and get to the ball in time.

Key Technique Develop fast footwork and lateral movement, along with explosive dives when needed.

Drill Agility ladders, short sprints, and reaction drills to enhance quickness and coordination.
